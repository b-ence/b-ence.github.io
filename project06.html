Album Art

Artwork created for music

Fubba-u-cubba-cubba yubba-obubba-ubba You-ba can subbabick my dadibbadick through a tuba

I love listening to music. I'm addicted to music. Sometimes the music I listen to has a lame cover art, or I just get inspired by the music itself, but in both occasions I always end up making my own cover for them.
I used to look at Sam Spratt's covers he made for Logic countless times, just wondering how he could even come up with these ideas. Nowadays I get asked to make art for singles and albums.
