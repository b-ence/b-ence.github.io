About
Me

Once an artist, always an artist

Hi, I'm Bence.
I've been drawing for as long as I can remember. As a kid, comic books and cartoons inspired me to pick up a pencil and fill every sheet of paper I could get my hands on. By the time I was seven, when people asked what I wanted to be when I grew up, I already knew the answer: an animator.

Life took me through many paths — eventually even into computer science — but that's a story for another day. After that detour, a borrowed drawing tablet and a simple sketch for a friend brought me back to art, and I haven't let go since.

These days, I train relentlessly, exploring every corner of visual art: painting, drawing, graphic design, photography, video editing, and even tattooing. My style shifts with the medium, ranging from inked, semi-realistic comic and manga-inspired drawings to highly detailed oil paintings.
