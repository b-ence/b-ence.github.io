Graphics Design

Logos, social media graphics, promotional materials

Optics

My journey in graphic design began in high school, when I started experimenting in Photoshop by creating fan covers for albums I admired. Over the years I expanded my skills from 2D design and photo manipulation to 3D work and, most recently, even pixel art. Along the way I've created logos, social media headers, posters, websites, and UI layouts for my own apps, while also taking on freelance projects that gave me the chance to apply this broad toolkit in professional settings.
