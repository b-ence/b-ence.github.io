Blanchey

So much purple

Purple.

If I had to list my least favorite colors, purple would be #1 for sure.

In contrast, Blanchey's favorite color is purple, thus he requested me to create a design for him strictly with royal purple and gold colors. See, I was very enthusiastic about the project itself up until the linework, but then I realized I had to work with a color combination I barely ever used before. This caused a bit of a trouble, because I had recreated some of the screens several times, due to me not liking the final outcome with colors the colors on. Eventually I figured out a process that eased my work and my worries were gone.

My biggest takeaway from the project is that sometimes you don't get to choose every aspect of the final product, and there's nothing wrong with that of course. Sometimes that is the only way to experiment with new things, and create new solutions.

See it in action here!
